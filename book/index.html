Attachment research spanning 50+ years tells us something that might surprise you. A parent's ability to reflect on their own life story is the strongest predictor of their child's security. Not income. Not education. Not parenting techniques.

That means the parent who had a difficult childhood but has processed it will raise a more secure child than the parent who had a "fine" childhood but never looked at it honestly.

"Your story is not a wound to fix. It is a resource to understand."
