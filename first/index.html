What Happens

The five days, plainly.

01

Day 1 — What's actually going on

First things first, let's understand your situation. We'll look at where things keep breaking down, what your teenager is up against right now, and what's actually driving the pattern. This conversation alone tends to bring insight.

02

Days 2, 3, 4 — One thing per day

I'll walk you through a specific part of my communication approach, one piece at a time. You'll practice it during our session so you're not going home to figure it out yourself. Expect to see a difference in how you approach the situation.

03

Day 5 — What changed

We look at what landed. What surprised you. What felt different. And we figure out honestly whether continuing to work together makes sense, or whether you have what you need to keep going on your own. Either way, I'm fine with it.
