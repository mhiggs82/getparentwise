How it works

Three phases. One transformative journey.

Each phase builds on the last. You don't rush to strategies before the foundation is solid.

1
Weeks 1 to 4

The Inside Out Audit

The work starts with you. Before you can shift the relationship, you need to understand what you bring into it, from your own adolescence, your patterns, and how you respond under pressure. Make sense of your own experiences of security and insecurity as a teenager.

  • Reflect on your origin story and how it shows up today
  • Build your personal response cycle for high-tension moments
2
Weeks 5 to 8

The Identity Base Builder

Now you learn to see your teen clearly. Adolescence is a period of intense internal change, and most parents are working from an outdated map. This phase gives you the right one.

  • Understand the dual identity shift happening in your teen right now
  • Learn exactly what adolescence requires and how to meet those needs
  • Create a culture of security your teen can rely on at home, in you and in the home
3
Weeks 9 to 12

The Teen Autonomy Blueprint

The goal was never to keep them close forever. It was to give them what they need to move forward securely. This phase empowers your teen to trust themselves in a way that strengthens, not severs, your relationship.

  • Become the guide on the side, not the authority above
  • Model emotional regulation so your teen learns by watching
  • Equip them with inner resilience they can trust
  • Transfer decision-making in a way that builds their confidence
